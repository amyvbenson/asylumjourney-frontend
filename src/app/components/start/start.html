#Kirkleeswelcomes Online Service Directory

This directory lists local, regional and national projects, organisations and services that can provide advice and support for asylum seekers and refugees.

It is designed to help officers and volunteers who are supporting refugees and asylum seekers in Kirklees. It covers all stages of the asylum process and all kinds of statutory and informal support, the Kirklees Welcomes Service Directory will help you provide support to individuals and families.

If you have any comments or feedback, or if you spot any gaps or errors, please contact Veronika and Stephanie on pathways@kcalc.org.uk.
